HUMIDITAS LUX

Innovative system for illumination of road marks when humidity is high (usually when it's raining or there is fog) in combination with dark at night.

This innovation would help with visibility of road marks in such conditions in places where it's most needed (e.g. lane merging).

Awards Contact Source

The Idea

The idea of project initialization is having multiple lights installed into road marks which will get triggered by only 1 station with sensors for night and humidity.

These lights would be connected via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth technology.

Environment

Humiditas Lux lights are designed to only turn on when triggered in necessary conditions. This targeted approach conserves energy and minimizes light pollution, protecting natural ecosystems and reducing disturbances to wildlife.

By embedding our lights directly into the road, we also create a durable and low-maintenance solution that requires fewer resources over time.

Solar panel usage (in version 2) are not only using sun as a power source and extending light lifespan but also reducing battery waste for overall safer environment.
